About Our Centre

Our Legacy

A Foundation Built on Faith

Established in 1998, the E G Muslim Community Centre began as a small gathering place for local families. Today, it stands as a beacon of Islamic values and community service, welcoming hundreds of worshippers every day.

Our mission is to foster a vibrant community that adheres to the teachings of the Quran and the Sunnah of Prophet Muhammad (PBUH), promoting peace, education, and social justice.
