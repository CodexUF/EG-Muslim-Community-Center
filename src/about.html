About Our Centre

Our Legacy

A Foundation Built on Faith

All praise is due to Allah, and may blessings and peace be upon His Messenger.

We would like to inform you that, due to the small number of Muslims in our East Grinstead area, a mosque has not yet been established here. Therefore, every Friday, we rent a hall to perform the Jumu’ah prayer.

In addition, we are making earnest efforts so that, very soon, we can build a permanent mosque here. We have firm faith that, by the infinite mercy and help of Allah, we will soon be able to establish a mosque in the East Grinstead area, insha’Allah.

May Allah accept this effort from us and make it easy. Ameen.
